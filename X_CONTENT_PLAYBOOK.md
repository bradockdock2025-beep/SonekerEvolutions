## PART 1 — Why You Have No Followers Yet (And How to Fix It)

### The real reason text-only posts don't grow:

X's algorithm heavily favors posts with **visual media** and **replies/engagement**. A text post from a new account is invisible. But the moment you add a screenshot of your product working, engagement multiplies 3–5x. And the moment you reply to big accounts consistently, people discover you.

### The 3 things that grow accounts in 2025:
1. **Show don't tell** — screenshot of Soneker output > any description of it
2. **Reply to large accounts** — your replies show up in their followers' feeds
3. **Consistency** — 1 post per day for 90 days beats 30 posts in one day then silence

## PART 8 — What "Building in Public" Actually Means

Building in public is NOT:
- Showing only the wins
- Vague updates ("making progress on the new feature!")
- Promotional posts disguised as updates

Building in public IS:
- Real numbers, including the bad ones
- Honest failures and what you learned
- Showing your thinking process, not just the result
- Asking your audience questions ("should I build X or Y next?")
- Celebrating small wins genuinely

**The formula that works:**
> Specific number + honest emotion + what you're doing about it

**Example:**
```
Last week: 3 new signups. Target was 50.

Honest reaction: it stings.

What I'm changing:
→ More screenshot demos (not just text)
→ Replying in r/productivity threads
→ Direct outreach to 10 potential users today

Back next week with the updated number.
```

This post will outperform any "we hit 1000 users!" post when you have 12 followers.
People root for honesty.
