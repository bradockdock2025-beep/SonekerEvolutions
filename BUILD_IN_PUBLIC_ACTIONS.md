## HOW TO MAKE QUICK GRAPHICS IN CANVA (FREE)

For posts where you need a text card, numbers card, or quote card:

1. Go to canva.com → free account
2. Click "Create design" → Custom size: **1200 × 675 px**
3. Background color: `#0a0818` (dark purple — Soneker brand)
4. Text color: white. Accent color: `#5B4EFF`
5. Bottom right corner: add `soneker.app` in small gray text
6. Export as PNG

You can make one of these in 5 minutes. Templates below for each post.
